Cookie Usage Details

This page explains what cookies Epic Bot uses, what they are for, and which are required for the site to function.

Cookies Used

  • authorization (Required)
    Used to remember your cookie consent choice ("allowed" or "denied"). This is required to avoid showing the cookie popup every visit.
  • Session cookies (backend) (Required for login/dashboard)
    Used to keep you logged in and securely identify your session when using the dashboard. These are set by the backend after Discord login and are essential for authentication.
  • Google Analytics cookies (Optional, third-party)
    Used by Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics and help improve the site. You can block these via browser settings or extensions.

Why We Use Cookies

  • To remember your cookie consent choice.
  • To keep you logged in and secure when using the dashboard.
  • To analyze site usage and improve the user experience (Google Analytics).

Which Cookies Are Required?

  • authorization and session cookies are required for basic functionality and login.
  • Google Analytics cookies are optional and not required for using the bot or dashboard.

If you have questions, see our Cookie Policy or contact support.
